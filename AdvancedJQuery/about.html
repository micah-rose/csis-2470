About Us

Dark Horse Investigations was founded in 1908 by Samuel Campbell. Since that time, Dark Horse Investigations has been family owned and operated. The business is now run by brothers Sam and Dean Winchester, four generation great grandsons of Samuel. Their dedication and outstanding teamwork have kept Dark Horse Investigations moving at top speed, with no sign of slowing down.
